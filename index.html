Visual Effects and Video Editing

I didn't think I would enjoy the video editing workflow as much as I do, but here I am doing it in my free time. You can see a sample of my work at the top of this website. I us Adobe Premiere Pro for basic video editing, After Effects for compositing, Fusion 360 and Blender for 3D modeling, Blender and Unreal Engine 4 for 3D rendering, and GIMP for photo editing.

3D Printing

I have a heavily modified Anet A8 which can be seen in the video at the top of the site. I've replaced the extruder with a Bowden based E3D volcano 6 clone. Tensioners have been added to all axies, all belts have been upgraded to more durable latex-based belts, and a servo mounted switch allows for automatic bed leveling. Speaking of the bed, I use a 200mm X 200mm sheet of glass for a print surface. I have to run it at a higher bed temperature than I would otherwise, but the smooth first layer and easy print removal is well worth it.
I also have a DaVinci Minimaker that is currently in the process of being "unlocked". The stock electronics use an RF-ID based system to restrict you to using only DaVinci brand filament, which is very over-priced. So, I am replacing the stock electronics with an open-source RAMPS system that allows the use of custom G-code, and is free of any artificial restrictions. Needless to say, the process has been less than seamless. I'll update this section when I get it printing.
